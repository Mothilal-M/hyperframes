05 / THE LOOP
— THE AGENT LOOP

Think → Act → Observe → Think → Answer.

THINK→
ACT→
OBSERVE→
THINK→
ANSWER

EXAMPLE · "REVIEW MY LANDING PAGE"

STEP 01User gives a goal: review this landing page.
STEP 02AI plans: I need copy, analytics, and feedback.
STEP 03AI acts: reads the page, fetches analytics, searches notes.
STEP 04AI observes: bounce is high, headline unclear, CTA too low.
STEP 05AI reasons again: not just design — also a messaging issue.
STEP 06AI answers with suggestions grounded in the actual data.
