# Visual Identity — Sample Demo

## Style Prompt

A minimal, type-driven motion design rooted in Swiss-grid principles: high-contrast monochrome canvas, a single saturated accent, and tight typographic hierarchy. Motion is precise — short distances, expo/power eases, no bounce. Negative space is structural.

## Colors

- `#0A0A0B` — canvas (near-black)
- `#F5F5F4` — primary text (warm off-white)
- `#9A9A98` — secondary text
- `#FF3B30` — accent (signal red)
- `#1F1F22` — surface lines / dividers

## Typography

- `Inter` — primary (display + UI), weights 400/600/800
- `JetBrains Mono` — labels, numerals, timestamps

## What NOT to Do

- No gradients on the canvas (banding under H.264)
- No drop shadows on text — use weight contrast instead
- No center-everything layouts; lean on a left-aligned grid
- No bouncy eases (`back.out`, `elastic`) — keep motion crisp
- No emojis or decorative chrome
