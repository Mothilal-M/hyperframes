# Visual Identity — Warm Editorial

## Style Prompt

A magazine-style editorial system. Paper-cream canvas with deep ink text, one terracotta accent, and an unapologetic display serif paired with clean sans body and a small-caps mono for labels. Generous negative space, asymmetric layouts, hairline rules instead of borders. Motion is slow and confident — long fades, gentle vertical drift, and crossfade transitions instead of hard wipes.

## Colors

- `#F4ECE0` — paper canvas (warm cream)
- `#1A1614` — ink (near-black with warm undertone)
- `#5A524A` — secondary text (warm gray)
- `#C75D2C` — terracotta accent
- `#E2D6C6` — divider / surface (slightly darker than canvas)
- `#7A6E5E` — caption / muted body

## Typography

- `Fraunces` — display serif (700–900) for titles, with optical sizing
- `Inter` — body and UI (400/500/600)
- `JetBrains Mono` — small-caps labels, page numbers, footnotes

## What NOT to Do

- No hard wipe transitions — use crossfades and gentle scale
- No saturated tech accents (no red, no neon)
- No grid HUD chrome — this is print-magazine, not dashboard
- No bouncy eases (`back.out`, `elastic`) — keep motion graceful
- No solid-fill cards everywhere — prefer hairline rules and breathing space
